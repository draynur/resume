Bio

Hometown: Armington, IL
Current: Champaign, IL

I come from a small farm town 30 miles SW of Illinois State University called Armington, IL (pop. 380). I grew up running around playing football and exploring the local creeks. My fascination with tech started young, as my older brother brought home a build-your-own RC car kit one day and I've been a tinkerer ever since. Nothing felt cooler to work on, and your product of hard work was a fun toy itself! Once I graduated high school I was lost over what to do. I took some time at the local community college and decided to study computer science, as I really enjoyed the introductory java language course that was taught. Over the last few years I have found myself being swept up in all things web development. I'm looking forward to any opportunity and can't wait to use my knowledge/experience.
